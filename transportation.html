Transportation

Due to the remote location of the wedding venue, there are two options to get there

Shuttle bus

We will provide a shuttle bus from Palma in the morning and return at night. If you decide to stay outside of Palma, please keep in mind that taxi services don’t really work around the island, so you will need a car to move around!

Driving to the venue

If you prefer, you can drive to Son Rullan and park at the venue. Parking is limited to 15/20 cars so please let us know if you plan to drive there to make sure we can accommodate everyone.

⚠️ Please let us know if you plan to drive!
