Where to stay

While there are many place to stay around the island, we recommend staying in Palma for a few reasons:

• Proximity to the airport

• We will provide a shuttle bus on the wedding day from Palma — see Transportation

• Large selection of hotels for all budgets

If you feel like exploring the island and want to stay in a more remote location we highly encourage it but we can’t guarantee transportation on the day. Keep in mind that taxi services don’t really work outside of Palma usually, so you will need a car to move around!
