Where to stay

Welcome to our guide on where to stay when visiting Mallorca for our wedding! In this page, we will provide you with information and recommendations on the best accommodation options available on the island. Whether you prefer luxury hotels, cozy bed and breakfasts, or vacation rentals, we've got you covered. Let's dive in and explore the wonderful places to stay in Mallorca! While there are many place to stay around the island, we recommend staying in Palma for a few reasons:

• Proximity to the airport

• We will provide a shuttle bus on the wedding day from Palma — see Transportation

• Large selection of hotels for all budgets

If you feel like exploring the island and want to stay in a more remote location we highly encourage it but we cant guarantee transportation on the day.
